About GrammarMON

GrammarMON is a retro-inspired educational RPG where players battle grammar-based creatures in a mysterious digital world. Train your mind, defeat corrupted knowledge, and uncover the secrets of the school.

Game Features

  • Turn-based battles with grammar creatures
  • Custom character progression
  • Online PvP mode and leaderboard
  • Retro graphics and keyboard-only controls
